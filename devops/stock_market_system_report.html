System Summary

The stock-market system combines player wallet balances, vault balances, portfolio holdings, world-news copy, and inbox-driven notifications into one shared backend. Aevora Exchange provides the trading terminal, while the main Pokemon Generations experience surfaces daily briefings and release news. Pokemon Center Admin acts as the command center for service startup, content control, and AI-assisted operations.

Core Financial Flows

  • Players execute buy and sell requests through `/economy/market/buy` and `/economy/market/sell`.
  • Wallet funds (`pokedollars`) cover trade execution, while long-term value sits in `user.bank.balance` and `user.bank.portfolio`.
  • Foreign or dimensional trades apply a link-tax envelope, creating themed cost pressure for cross-market activity.
  • Deposit and withdraw routes bridge liquid wallet funds and the Silph-Gold vault system.
  • Fortune ranking and portfolio views synthesize wallet, bank, and holdings value for player-facing status screens.

Player Messaging Layer

  • Daily login briefings deliver one message per player per day with dedupe protection.
  • Trade confirmation mail can be generated automatically after buy and sell events.
  • Low-vault balance alerts warn players when bank reserves fall below configured thresholds.
  • Shared home news remains distinct from market-specific reporting to avoid overlap.

AI Expansion Opportunities

  • Investor digests: summarize player holdings and recent trade behavior into inbox-ready portfolio briefs.
  • Market explainers: convert raw price movement into short in-world commentary for the ticker and admin reports.
  • Compliance filters: scan outgoing mail and market posts for policy issues before release.
  • Support acceleration: draft player-support replies around failed trades, missing funds, or onboarding confusion.
  • Retention loops: tailor daily login briefings and alerts based on player wealth, region, or recent activity.
