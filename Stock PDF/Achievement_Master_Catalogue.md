### 🛡️ How to Earn Badges
1.  **Sync**: Ensure your terminal is connected to the master server.
2.  **Execution**: Requirements are tracked in real-time.
3.  **Claim**: Payouts are automatically deposited into your Bank Balance upon achievement.

> [!TIP]
> Keep an eye on the **Discovery Grid** in your Profile. Some achievements are "Secret" and will only appear once the threshold is crossed!
