## 🕯️ VII. Professional Charting Manual
The Aevora Terminal provides professional-grade visual intelligence:

### 📊 1-Minute Candlestick Charts
Each "Candle" on the graph represents **60 seconds** of market movement:
- **Neon-Green Body**: Price closed **higher** than it opened.
- **Error-Red Body**: Price closed **lower** than it opened.
- **Wicks (Vertical Lines)**: Represent the **High** and **Low** price achieved during that 1-minute window.

4.  **Execute**: Tap an asset to analyze the **Candlestick History**, then select **BUY_SHARES**.
